Baked Portobellos
Step Precedure Observation
1 Sharp Knife Carefully slide both sides of the knife over the whetstone
2 Wash Portobellos Cut off rotten parts. Make sure dirt is gone
3 Cut Portobellos Dry them. Make Vertical slides of approximately 0.5 inch
4 Spice & Bake Organice them over an oven plate -also use butter paper- Olive oil + Salt + Pepper + Merken. Bake 8' at 392°F
